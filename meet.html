let's meet

Shedule a meeting with Dr. Busta


  1. Go to this link: Meeting Calendar for Dr. Busta.

  2. Click the arrow button in the top left corner of the calendar to select the week you would like to meet. You should see lists of 30-minute appointments.

  3. Click on the button for the appointment you would like to book.

  4. Change the "What" description in the dialog box to your name (first & last).

  5. Once you add your appointment, I'll get an email, and we will meet at that time in my office, HCAMS 308.

  6. If you must cancel an appointment, let me know ASAP so I can free up the time for another student.
